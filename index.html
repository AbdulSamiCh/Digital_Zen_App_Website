Welcome to Digital Zen

Digital Zen is a friendly app designed to help you find a healthier balance with your devices. We all know how easy it is to get lost in the digital world, missing out on real moments. Digital Zen helps you track your phone time, reminds you to take breaks, and even lets you focus without distractions.

Features

Digital Zen is equipped with lots of features to help you reduce social media intake

Time Outs

Smart feature that helps reduce usage of scrolling behaviour

To-do

Built in to do to help you get things done while you are on social media break.

Work Mode

Allows you to focus on your work and be more productive

Notes

Quickly write notes while you are focusing on your work.

Sleep Reminders

Sleep reminders and auto shut down of social media apps

Statistics

Stats to let you know how much time you are spending on screens

Zen Assist

Gradually reduces your social media intake with smart algorithm.
